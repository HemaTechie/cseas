About CSEA

The Computer Science and Engineering Association (CSEA) is a student-run body that fosters innovation, technical expertise, and collaboration among students. We organize events, workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures to enhance the skill sets of our members and prepare them for the challenges of the tech industry.
